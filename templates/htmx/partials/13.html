So what are the drawbacks?

Locality of Behaviour - This is a double edged sword. On the one hand, all of the code relating to a specific part of your frontend functionality is all in the same space, but on the other your HTML templates are going to get big

  • Tailwind: You will find that you are repeating yourself and reusing the same classes over and over again. Make the most of template looping.
  • AlpineJS: Can result in a much larger templates as your functionality becomes more complex.
  • HTMX You need to think about back button behaviour and what to do when a full page load hits an end point that is returning HTML partials.i You also loose some information transfer in the request, such as messages.
